ABOUT US

China National Decoration Co., LTD. was established in 1984 with the approval of the State Economic Commission and the Ministry of Light Industry. It is the first batch of large-scale and high-grade decoration enterprises with Grade A qualification of indoor and outdoor building decoration construction and design. Large-scale cross-regional, cross-industry digital assembly type construction joint-stock decoration backbone enterprises.

BUSINESS DISPLAY

Architectural Decoration

China Decoration has been deeply engaged in the field of building decoration for nearly 40 years, and has been selected as one of the "Top 100 Enterprises of China's building decoration Industry Comprehensive data Statistics" for many consecutive years. It has undertaken many key projects and high-quality projects, and has the design and construction service capability of the whole industry chain such as interior design, building curtain wall, soft decoration art and intelligent assembly.
